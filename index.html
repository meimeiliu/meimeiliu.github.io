Education

I am working with Prof. David B. Dunson as a post-doc in Department of Statistics at Duke University, where I develop methodology for nonparametric Bayesian, machine learning, and neural imaging. I was a Ph.D. student in Purdue University, where I was advised by Prof. Guang Cheng and Prof. Zuofeng Shang. I received a M.S. in statistics at University of Science and Technology of China, where I was supervised by Prof. Weiping Zhang.
